West African Peanut Soup

Recipe from Cookie & Kate

Peanut soup is a great recipe for those lazy days when you just want to curl up on the couch in your pajamas and watch terrible TV. No shame, we've all been there. But peanut soup can be a real staple to your weekly meal plan.

Ingredients

Measurement Item Prepared
6 cups Low-sodium vegetable broth  
1 Medium red onion Chopped
4 Garlic cloves Minced
2 tbsp Fresh ginger Peeled and minced
1 tsp Salt  
1 bunch Collard greens or kale Ribs remobed and leaves chopped into 1" strips
3/4 cup Unsalted peanut butter  
1/2 cup Tomato paste  
To taste Hot sauce or sriracha  
1/4 cup Peanuts Roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. In a medium stock pot, bring the vegetable broth to a boil.
  2. Add onion, ginger, garlic, and salt.
  3. Cook on medium-low heat for 20 minutes.
  4. In a medium-sized, heat-safe mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter and tomato paste.
  5. Whisk the mixture together until smooth.
  6. Add the mixture to the soup after the 20 minutes have passed and whisk to break clumps.
  7. Stir in the collard greens or kale.
  8. Add hot sauce to taste.
  9. Simmer soup for 15 minutes on medium-low heat, stirring often.
  10. Serve over cooked brown rice or with bread, and top with chopped peanuts.
