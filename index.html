West African Peanut Soup

Recipe from Cookie & Kate

Peanut soup is a great recipe for those lazy days when you just want to curl up on the couch in your pajamas and watch terrible TV. No shame, we've all been there. But peanut soup can be a real staple to your weekly meal plan.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons peeled and minced fresh ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 bunch collard greens or kale, ribs removed and leaves chopped into 1-inch strips
  • 3/4 cup unsalted peanut butter (chunky or smooth)
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • Hot sauce, like sriracha
  • 1/4 cup roughly chopped peanuts, for garnish
